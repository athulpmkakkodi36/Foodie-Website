About Us

Fooday Story

With an enviable culinary heritage dating back to more than seven decades the Foodie Group has set new benchmarks in the restaurant industry. The flagship Foodie restaurant is a true icon of Calicut and the Foodie restaurant in Dubai is one of the most popular restaurants in the UAE.

Acclaimed as india's best restaurant for coastal cuisine (Times Now Foodie Award) and consistently ranked among Dubai's best restaurants(TimeOut Awards and Ahlan Masala Awards), Foodie continues to set the highest standard of taste and service. We hope you have a Foodie experience filled with unmatched taste, delightful contentment and memories to cherish. Bon Appetit

Meet Our

Master Chef

Sabu

Sabu is an Emmy award-winning chef who owns three California restaurants and also licenses out his name and infamous “Flavortown” catchphrase to restaurants across the country. His name carries so much weight because of his two long-running TV Shows, Diners, Drive-Ins, and Dives and Guy’s Grocery Games. He even hosted the non-cooking tv game show Minute To Win It for two seasons because of his charismatic personality. This is why he’s not just one of the most famous chefs, but maybe the most famous chef on TV.
